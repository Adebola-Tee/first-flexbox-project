For the purposes of diagnosis, patient care, treatment, and improving a
person's health, medical technology refers to technologies,
i.e., gadgets, that are used in healthcare systems.

The healthcare industry uses this sector to link technology and patient care. All of the instruments,
apparatus, and gadgets used in patient diagnosis and treatment are provided by MedTech.

History

It is possible to trace the development of medical technology to the early understanding of diseases and infections.

The Greek physician Hippocrate established a connection between anatomical and chemical laboratory data and the etiologies of illnesses about 460 B.C.
